# Warband (2d6+6 years)
You were brought up in a Chim’onn warband.

* **Prerequisites**: Chim’onn
* **Attributes**: STR +1, AGI +1, END +1, LUC +1
* **Skill Choices**: [Physical], survival, leadership, brawling, carousing

## Exploits
**Scrapper**. Life in the warband required constant competition for position and rank, which has made you fierce and wary, giving you a +1d6 bonus to intimidation checks.  
**Frontier-forged**. A hard upbringing on the edge of civilization has made you resourceful in the wild. You gain a +1d6 bonus to survival checks made to get along in the wild.
